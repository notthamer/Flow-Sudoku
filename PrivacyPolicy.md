# Privacy Policy for Flow Sudoku

**Last Updated: November 16, 2025**

## Introduction

Flow Sudoku ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our macOS application.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

- **Account Information** (Optional): If you choose to create an account, we collect your email address and encrypted password.
- **Session Data**: We store your Sudoku game sessions locally on your device, including:
  - Puzzle difficulty and completion status
  - Time spent on puzzles
  - Notes written in the declutter section
  - Session timestamps
  - Mistake counts

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Statistics**: We collect anonymous usage data to improve the app, including:
  - Daily session counts
  - Feature usage
  - App crash reports (if any)
- **Device Information**: Basic device information for app functionality (macOS version, screen size)

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

- Provide and maintain the app's core functionality
- Save and sync your session history (if you're signed in)
- Track daily usage limits for Free tier users
- Improve app performance and user experience
- Provide customer support when requested

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage

- All session data is stored locally on your device by default
- Data is saved using macOS standard storage mechanisms (UserDefaults and file system)
- No data leaves your device unless you sign in and enable cloud sync

### Cloud Sync (Optional)

- If you create an account and sign in, your session data can be synced to our servers
- All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols (HTTPS)
- Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
- You can delete your account and all associated data at any time

## Data Sharing

We do NOT:

- Sell your personal information to third parties
- Share your session notes or declutter writing with anyone
- Use your data for advertising purposes
- Track you across other apps or websites

We MAY share information only in these circumstances:

- If required by law or legal process
- To protect our rights, safety, or property
- With service providers who help us operate the app (under strict confidentiality agreements)

## Your Rights

You have the right to:

- Access your personal data
- Delete your account and all associated data
- Export your session history
- Opt out of cloud sync (use the app offline)
- Decline to create an account (use as a guest)

## Children's Privacy

Flow Sudoku is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected such information, please contact us to have it removed.

## Free vs. Studio Versions

- **Free Version** (App Store): Limited to 1 session per day, data stored locally unless you sign in
- **Studio Version** (Downloaded from website): Unlimited usage, payment processed externally

The Free version does not include any in-app purchases. Upgrading to Studio requires downloading a separate version from our website.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

- Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
- Updating the "Last Updated" date
- Sending an email if you have an account (for significant changes)

Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

## Data Retention

- Local session data: Retained until you manually delete it or uninstall the app
- Cloud data (if synced): Retained until you delete your account
- Account data: Deleted within 30 days of account deletion request
