## Description

**Transform your morning routine with Flow Sudoku — the focus ritual that prepares your mind for deep work.**

Flow Sudoku isn't just a puzzle app. It's a deliberate practice designed to help you enter a flow state and start your day with clarity and intention.

### THE FLOW RITUAL

1. **Warm Up Your Brain** — Solve carefully crafted Sudoku puzzles to activate logical thinking and pattern recognition
2. **Declutter Your Mind** — Use the integrated writing space to clear mental noise and surface what's really on your mind
3. **Enter Flow State** — Start your work with focus, clarity, and purpose

### WHY FLOW SUDOKU?

**Minimalist Design** — Clean, distraction-free interface that keeps you focused on the ritual
**Progressive Difficulty** — Easy, Medium, and Hard puzzles to match your skill level
**Declutter Writing** — Dedicated space to process thoughts, anxieties, and ideas alongside your puzzle
**Session History** — Track your progress and reflect on past sessions
**No Distractions** — No ads, no notifications, no social features — just you and your focus ritual

### FREE TIER INCLUDES

- 1 complete session per day
- All difficulty levels
- Full Sudoku features (notes, undo, hints)
- Declutter writing
- Session history
- Clean, minimalist interface

### HOW TO USE

Start each morning with a Flow Sudoku session:
- Choose your difficulty level
- Set your session duration
- Solve the puzzle while decluttering your mind
- Begin your day with clarity

### BUILT FOR FOCUS

Flow Sudoku is designed for knowledge workers, creatives, students, and anyone who needs to think clearly. Use it as a morning ritual, a pre-work warm-up, or a mental reset during your day.

### UNLIMITED SESSIONS

Want more than one session per day? Visit flowsudoku.com for unlimited access.
